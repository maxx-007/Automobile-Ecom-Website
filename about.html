Our Mission

At TopSecret, our mission is to provide high-quality automotive parts and performance upgrades. We are dedicated to enhancing your driving experience with top-of-the-line products and exceptional customer service.

Our Story

Top Secret, founded by Smokey Nagata in 1996, is a renowned Japanese tuning company known for its high-performance modifications of sports cars, particularly those from Japanese manufacturers like Toyota, Nissan, and Subaru. The company has built a strong reputation for pushing the limits of car performance with extreme tuning, setting records, and gaining widespread media attention. Smokey Nagata, also known for his street racing prowess, has made Top Secret a prominent name in the automotive tuning world through innovative projects and a commitment to exceptional vehicle performance.Krish Hadkar who is an Third Year Engineering student at KJ Somaiya College of Engineering from India has joined Smokey's team as the Cheif Technical officer. He manages and overlooks on all the technical tuning and builds maps of various hypercars.
